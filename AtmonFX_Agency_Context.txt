3. SERVICE VERTICALS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

AtmonFX operates across five core verticals:

1. BRAND CGI
   - Photorealistic product commercials for D2C and consumer brands
   - CGI brand films and motion visuals
   - Product launches, hero shots, advertising campaigns
   - Target clients: D2C brands, FMCG companies, consumer electronics, cosmetics,
     fashion labels, lifestyle brands
   - Why us: We deliver ad-agency-quality CGI at a fraction of traditional
     production house costs

2. OTT / FILM VFX
   - Visual effects for OTT series (streaming platforms: Netflix, SonyLIV,
     Amazon Prime, Disney+ Hotstar, Zee5, JioCinema)
   - Film VFX for mid-budget Bollywood and regional productions
   - Environment creation, compositing, creature work, title sequences
   - Target clients: OTT production houses, independent film producers,
     post-production studios

3. ARCHVIZ (Architectural Visualization)
   - Photorealistic architectural renders and animations
   - Real-time interactive walkthroughs built in Unreal Engine 5
   - Clients can explore a space in real time, change materials on the fly,
     and see lighting at different times of day — before construction begins
   - Target clients: Real estate developers, architecture firms, ArchViz studios
     looking to upgrade their pipeline

4. GAMING ASSETS
   - Game-ready 3D assets, character modeling, environment work
   - Support for indie studios and mid-size game developers
   - Blender pipeline optimized for game engine export
   - Target clients: Indian indie game studios, mobile game companies,
     international studios looking for outsourced asset production

5. PRODUCT VISUALIZATION
   - Studio-quality product renders for e-commerce, advertising, and packaging
   - 360-degree product spins, exploded views, material studies
   - Target clients: E-commerce brands, manufacturers, packaging agencies

4. IDEAL CLIENT PROFILE (ICP)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

HIGH-PRIORITY TARGET SEGMENTS:

  D2C Consumer Brands (Brand CGI)
    - D2C brands with strong digital/social presence
    - Currently running live-action ads with no CGI vendor visible
    - Have active product launches, festive campaigns, or rebranding in progress
    - Budget signals: Funded startup, mid-market brand, or publicly listed FMCG
    - Key roles to target: Marketing Head, Brand Manager, Creative Director,
      VP Marketing, Founder/Co-Founder

  OTT / Film Production Houses
    - Production houses actively creating content for streaming platforms
    - Shows/films with mid-to-large VFX requirements
    - Projects with announced production or recently greenlit
    - Key roles: VP Production, Post Production Head, VFX Supervisor,
      Line Producer, Executive Producer

  ArchViz & Real Estate Firms
    - Architecture firms and real estate developers who still deliver static renders
    - Studios open to upgrading to real-time Unreal Engine pipeline
    - Key roles: Founder, Creative Director, Head of Visualization

  Game Studios
    - Indie and mid-size studios with active development pipelines
    - Looking for 3D generalist support on a project or retainer basis
    - Key roles: Game Director, Art Director, Studio Head

ICP SCORING DIMENSIONS (used in Lead Engine):
  - Budget signals (funding, revenue, active campaigns)
  - Active project evidence (recent product launches, announced productions)
  - No visible CGI vendor / outsources work
  - Relevance to AtmonFX's verticals
  - Decision-maker accessibility (are we contacting the right person?)
  - Geography preference: India-first, with international as secondary

ICP SCORE SCALE: 0–100
  85–100 : Hot lead — prioritize immediately
  70–84  : Warm — worth contacting with personalization
  Below 70: Lower priority or niche mismatch

6. OUTREACH SYSTEM & METHODOLOGY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

APPROACH:
  Quality over quantity. 50–100 well-researched, personalized outreaches will
  always outperform a spray-and-pray blast to thousands.

THE SPEC PROJECT PLAY (Unique Differentiator):
  Before contacting a brand, Vishal builds a 15–30 second speculative CGI spot
  for their product category. Dev presents it in the outreach. This transforms
  the pitch from "trust us" to "look what we already made for you." This is
  AtmonFX's core unfair advantage as a new studio.

OUTREACH CHANNELS:
  - Cold email (primary): dev@atmonfx.com via Namecheap Private Email
  - LinkedIn DM (secondary): Direct messages on LinkedIn
  - Future scale: Instantly.ai (planned for 50+ sends/week)

FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE (4-touch system):
  Day 0  — First Touch: Personalized cold email or LinkedIn DM
  Day 4  — Gentle Nudge: Brief follow-up, offer to send asset directly
  Day 10 — Value Drop: Loom breakdown video showing process
  Day 18 — Break-up + Pivot: Final email, leave on high note, ask for referral

PIPELINE STAGES:
  0 — Research    (identified, not yet contacted)
  1 — Contacted   (first touch sent)
  2 — Replied     (prospect has responded)
  3 — In Discussion (active conversation / discovery underway)
  4 — Closed      (project won)

REALISTIC BENCHMARKS:
  Open Rate (cold email, personalized) : 35–50%
  Reply Rate                           : 8–15%
  Discovery Call Rate                  : 3–7%
  Close Rate (from discovery calls)    : 20–40%

DEV'S WEEKLY OUTREACH RHYTHM:
  Monday    — Research 10 new prospects, score them against ICP
  Tuesday   — Send 5–7 personalized first touches
  Wednesday — Follow up on Day-4 prospects
  Thursday  — LinkedIn engagement (comment, connect, warm up)
  Friday    — Review pipeline, log responses, plan next week
